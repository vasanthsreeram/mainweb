About Me

Hello! I'm Vasanth, a bioengineering student at NTU Singapore with a deep passion for both Engineering and entrepreneurship. I specialize in tech, with a focus on biological systems. My journey extends beyond academics into the realm of startups, where I'm a co-founder of Bugboom, a value-added service-based company bringing sustainable food-waste management to Singapore using Black Soldier Flies (BSF).

With experience running a tuition startup, I've developed effective teaching methods that blend technology-based and inquiry-based learning. My expertise also includes Arduino projects and developing SOPs for efficient operations.

Outside of work, I enjoy engaging in various hobbies, from bouldering to working on innovative solutions that can positively impact the world.

I'm always excited to connect with like-minded individuals and explore new opportunities to make a positive impact.
